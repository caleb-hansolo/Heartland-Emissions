From 1990, Nebraska's emissions have steadily increased. Gradual growth in emissions from the Industrial, Transportation, and especially Electric Power Sectors have been the main contributors, with Electric Power doubling its emissions from 1990 to 2014, before slowly receding again.

In fact, Nebraska is only one of 12 US states to have increased their yearly emissions since 2005 (Source 2)
